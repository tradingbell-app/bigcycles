Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Short version

👁️ What we collect — almost nothing

TradingBell does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers. The app works entirely on your device.

Payments are processed securely by Apple. We never see your payment card details.

📦 Information We Collect

1. No account, no profile. TradingBell has no sign-up or login flow. All your settings (theme, selected exchanges, alarms, favorites) are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults / Core Data). Nothing is sent to our servers.

2. In-App Purchases. If you subscribe to TradingBell Premium, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive only a non-personal receipt confirmation to verify your subscription status — no payment method details ever reach us.

3. Notifications. TradingBell may ask permission to send you local push notifications for market open/close alarms you configure. These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve sending data to any server.

4. Crash reports & diagnostics. If you have opted in to sharing analytics with developers in your iPhone settings, Apple may share anonymised crash reports with us. This data does not include any personally identifiable information.

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any contact details
  • Location data (GPS or IP-based)
  • Financial data, trading accounts, or portfolio information
  • Browsing history or activity outside the app
  • Device contacts, photos, or camera access
  • Any behavioural analytics or advertising identifiers

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs (e.g. Google Ads, Meta) and we do not sell or share data with third parties for marketing purposes.

🤝 Third-Party Services

Apple Inc. — The App Store manages all payments, subscription management, and (optionally) crash reporting. Apple's privacy policy governs how Apple handles this data: apple.com/privacy.

iCloud (Apple Inc.) — If you are signed into iCloud, the app may sync your preferences and settings across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored on Apple's servers under your Apple ID and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device's Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

We do not embed any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or any other third-party SDKs that collect personal data.

🗄️ Data Storage & Security

All app data (preferences, alarms, favourites) lives on your device. If you have iCloud Backup or iCloud Sync enabled, this data may be stored on Apple's iCloud servers. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling TradingBell from your device.

We do not operate any servers that store personal user data. We cannot access the data stored in your iCloud account.

🇪🇺 European Users — GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access — You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure — You may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction — You may request that we restrict processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability — You may request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

Because TradingBell collects virtually no personal data and stores everything locally on your device, the most effective way to exercise these rights is to uninstall the app and/or disable iCloud Sync. For any other requests, contact us at bigcycles.app@gmail.com.

🇺🇸 California Users — CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to know — You may request information about personal data we collect, use, disclose, or sell.
  • Right to delete — You may request deletion of personal data we have collected from you.
  • Right to opt-out of sale — We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data for cross-context behavioural advertising.
  • Right to non-discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at bigcycles.app@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days.

👶 Children's Privacy

TradingBell is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided personal information to us, please contact us at bigcycles.app@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete it promptly.
